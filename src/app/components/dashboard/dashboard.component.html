This is what's next

Risking burnout is a common challenge of ministry leaders, so know you are not alone. Here are some steps you need to take to combat burnout: Learn to pace yourself: not everything needs to be done. Just because someone has an emergency doesn’t mean it needs to become your emergency. Focus on the things you need to and don’t take on more than you can handle. Delegate to others: Do not be afraid to hand things off to others and allow them to take some of the load. You do not need to do everything, and you shouldn’t. Hand things off to allow others to shine and live out their calling. Take a vacation: If you haven’t taken one in awhile you are way overdue.
